Part 1 - English Article

Police Captain Detained After Fatal Shooting of Wife Outside Buriram Fitness Centre; Trainers Restrained Armed Suspect at the Scene

A police captain in Buriram province was detained after allegedly opening fire outside a two-storey fitness centre on June 17, killing his wife and injuring her mother. The article was published by The Thaiger on June 18, 2026, and cited reports from ThaiRath, Channel 8, Nang Rong Police Station, and local rescue workers.

Officers from Nang Rong Police Station and rescue workers from the Siam Ruamjai Pu-in Foundation responded to the incident at about 3.20pm behind Sra Ya Ma Market. The deceased woman was identified as 37-year-old Treeraya, also known as Bam. She was found with a gunshot wound to the head, and rescue workers were unable to save her.

Nearby, Treeraya's mother, 58-year-old Mantana, was found with a gunshot wound to the shoulder. She was transported to hospital for treatment and was later reported to be in stable condition. Police identified the suspect as Police Captain Teerapong.

According to the report, Teerapong attempted to leave the scene, but two fitness trainers restrained him until officers arrived. Video footage shared by Channel 8 showed one trainer holding the gunman from behind while another struck him in an effort to prevent him from escaping. Their actions received widespread praise on Thai social media because they had confronted an armed suspect at close range.

A witness told ThaiRath that she saw the accused place an arm around Mantana's neck before allegedly shooting her in the shoulder. The witness further claimed that he then called out to Treeraya before allegedly shooting her in the head. Reports stated that Treeraya and Teerapong had two children, aged nine and five.

The couple had reportedly lived separately for more than three years but had not completed formal divorce proceedings. Treeraya had sought a divorce, while the officer allegedly opposed the separation. Police initially suspected jealousy may have been a factor, although the investigation is ongoing.

Source: The Thaiger, "Fitness trainers restrain police who fatally shoots wife in Buriram," published June 18, 2026.
